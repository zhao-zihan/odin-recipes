Cauliflower Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Cauliflower chicken stir fry is a traditional Chinese family dish. It is easy to cook and very healthy. It's a must try for chicken lovers!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of chicken breast
  • 1 pound of cauliflower
  • one tomato
  • corn (optional)
  • two tbsp of olive oil
  • one tbsp of minced garlic
  • one tbsp of salt
  • one tbsp of soy sauce
  • one tbsp of bean paste
  • one tbsp of cooking wine
  • one tbsp of ground white pepper
  • 200 ml water

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces the size of bottle caps.
  2. Marinate the chicken with cooking wine, salt, and white pepper and sit for 25 minutes.
  3. Chop the cauliflower into to small pieces and briefly blanch them.
  4. Heat a deep pan and pour in the olive ol, add the bean paste once the oil is warmed up.
  5. Important step! slowly stir the bean paste til you can smell the rich flavour from the sauce.
  6. Add the cauliflower and mix it with minced garlic, stir fry til everything mixed up well.
  7. Pour in the chicken, stir fry til the meat turns white and then drop some soy sauce.
  8. Add one chopped tomato and 200 ml water, turn to medium heat and cover the pot, let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. The dish is ready. Enjoy!
