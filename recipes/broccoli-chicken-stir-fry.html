Broccoli chicken stir fry

Description

Broccoli chicken stir fry is a traditional Chinese family dish. It is easy to cook and very healthy. It's a must try for chicken lovers!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of chicken breast
  • 1 pound of broccoli
  • one tomato
  • corn (optional)
  • one tablespoon of minced garlic
  • one tablespoon of soy sauce
  • one tablespoon of bean paste
